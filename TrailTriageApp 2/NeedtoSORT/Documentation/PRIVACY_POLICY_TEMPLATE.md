# Privacy Policy for TrailTriage

**Effective Date: November 10, 2025**  
**Last Updated: November 10, 2025**

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to TrailTriage. This Privacy Policy explains how Black Elk Mountain Medicine ("we," "us," "our") handles information in connection with the TrailTriage mobile application (the "App").

TrailTriage is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your medical documentation should remain private and under your control.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

TrailTriage stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

- **SOAP Notes**: Patient assessments, vital signs, treatment notes, and incident documentation you create
- **Responder Information**: Your name, agency, rescue number, and certifications (if you choose to provide them)
- **App Settings**: Your preferences for note-taking and display options
- **Reference Bookmarks**: Protocols and glossary entries you bookmark for quick access

**Important**: This data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework on your device and is **never transmitted to our servers or any third parties**.

### 2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit:
- ❌ Usage analytics or tracking data
- ❌ Device identifiers
- ❌ Location data (unless you manually enter it into a note)
- ❌ Contact lists or personal contacts
- ❌ Photos (unless you choose to attach them to notes)
- ❌ Any personally identifiable information beyond what you enter

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Local Storage Only

All information you create in TrailTriage remains on your device. We use this information solely to:
- Display your SOAP notes and reference materials
- Enable search and organization features
- Generate PDF exports when you choose to share notes
- Restore your data if you reinstall the app (via iCloud backup, if enabled)

### 3.2 No Server Transmission

TrailTriage is designed to work **100% offline**. We do not:
- Upload your notes to any cloud servers
- Sync data across devices (unless you use iCloud, managed by Apple)
- Share data with third-party services
- Collect analytics or crash reports

---

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Your Control

You have complete control over your data:
- **Export**: You can export SOAP notes as PDFs using Apple's native sharing system
- **Delete**: You can delete any note at any time
- **Share**: You control who receives exported notes through standard iOS sharing

### 4.2 We Do Not Sell Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or SOAP notes to third parties for any purpose.

### 4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, such as in response to:
- Valid legal process (subpoena, court order)
- Enforcement of our terms of service
- Protection of our rights, property, or safety

However, because we do not collect or store your data on our servers, we typically have no data to disclose.

---

## 5. Data Security

### 5.1 Device Security

Your data is protected by:
- **iOS Security**: Apple's industry-leading device encryption
- **Local Storage**: Data never leaves your device unless you export it
- **No Cloud Dependency**: No vulnerability to server breaches

### 5.2 Your Responsibility

We recommend:
- Use a strong device passcode or biometric authentication
- Keep your iOS updated for latest security patches
- Be mindful when sharing exported PDFs
- Back up your device regularly

---

## 6. Third-Party Services

### 6.1 Apple Services

TrailTriage uses Apple's built-in frameworks:
- **SwiftData**: For local data storage
- **PDFKit**: For PDF generation
- **iOS Sharing**: For exporting notes

These are Apple's native services and subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

### 6.2 In-App Purchases

If you subscribe to TrailTriage Pro:
- Payments are processed by Apple's App Store
- We receive only confirmation that you have an active subscription
- No payment information is shared with us
- Subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/)

## 7. Children's Privacy

TrailTriage is intended for medical professionals and individuals trained in wilderness medicine. The App is not directed to children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## 8. International Users

TrailTriage is available worldwide. By using the App, you consent to having your data stored locally on your device in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:
- **Right to Access**: You can access all your data within the App
- **Right to Erasure**: You can delete any or all data at any time
- **Right to Portability**: You can export your data as PDF
- **No Automated Decision-Making**: We do not use automated profiling

---

## 9. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- **Right to Know**: We've disclosed what information we collect above (all stored locally)
- **Right to Delete**: You can delete all data by deleting notes or uninstalling the App
- **Right to Opt-Out of Sale**: We do not sell your personal information
- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: We do not discriminate based on privacy choices

To exercise these rights, you can manage your data directly in the App or contact us at support@blackelkmountainmedicine.com.

## 11. Data Retention

### 11.1 How Long We Keep Data

We do not keep any data because all data is stored locally on your device. You control retention:
- **Active Notes**: Remain on device until you delete them
- **Archived Notes**: Remain on device until you delete them
- **App Settings**: Remain until you change them or uninstall the App

### 11.2 Upon Uninstallation

When you uninstall TrailTriage:
- All local data is deleted from your device
- If you have iCloud backup enabled, Apple may retain a backup per their policy
- We have no copy of your data on our servers
